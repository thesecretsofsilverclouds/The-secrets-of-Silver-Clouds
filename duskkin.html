Duskkin

Race Faction

Summary

Cybernetic vampires blending ancient bloodlust with cutting-edge technology

Description

The Duskkin represent the dark evolution of vampirism into the technological age. Part ancient curse, part technological augmentation, they are vampires who have embraced cybernetic enhancement as a means to overcome their traditional weaknesses and amplify their supernatural strengths.

Origins

The Duskkin emerged in the late 20th century when a cabal of vampires, weary of hiding from humanity's advancing technology, decided to meet progress on its own terms. Through brutal experimentation and the sacrifice of countless subjects, they developed methods to integrate cybernetics with undead flesh without rejection.

The process is agonizing. Vampiric regeneration fights against the implants even as it bonds with them, creating a hybrid physiology that is neither fully organic nor purely mechanical. Those who survive transformation gain abilities far beyond traditional vampires, but at the cost of their remaining humanity.

Abilities & Enhancements

Duskkin retain traditional vampiric powers—enhanced strength, speed, and regeneration—while adding technological augmentation. Optical implants allow them to perceive multiple spectrums of light, negating the darkness limitation that hampered their ancestors. Dermal plating provides protection against the weaknesses that once plagued their kind.

Many Duskkin incorporate weapons directly into their bodies. Retractable claws with monomolecular edges, integrated firearms, and energy drains that siphon life force through touch make them formidable combatants. Some possess implants that allow interfacing with electronic systems, turning modern infrastructure into extensions of their senses.

Society & Culture

Duskkin society operates through a brutal meritocracy. Strength, cunning, and technological sophistication determine rank. They view traditional vampires as relics clinging to obsolete limitations, while pure humans are seen as either resources or threats depending on their utility.

They have established hidden enclaves throughout London's underground, where they can operate beyond the scrutiny of groups like MI6 and The Church. These territories serve as both sanctuaries and laboratories, where Duskkin continue pushing the boundaries of technomagical enhancement.

Threat Level

Every major supernatural faction considers Duskkin a significant threat. Their combination of vampiric powers and technological advancement makes them dangerous opponents. Their willingness to use violence without hesitation, coupled with their view of humans as prey, puts them in conflict with anyone attempting to maintain peace between supernatural and mortal worlds.
