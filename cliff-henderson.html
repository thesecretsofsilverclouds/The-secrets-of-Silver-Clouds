Cliff Henderson

Character MI6

Summary

MI6's General—a man who turned personal tragedy into purpose, mentoring the next generation while commanding the earth

Description

The General. Cliff Henderson commands MI6 with the kind of presence that makes rookies stand straighter and veterans nod in respect. Serious when duty calls, but blessed with an unexpectedly sharp sense of humour that cuts through tension like his Holy Item cuts through stone.

Background

His reputation: formidable. His secret: he finds the most fulfilment not in strategic victories or political manoeuvring, but in mentoring young agents—guiding them, protecting them, seeing them grow. There's a reason for that. His daughter would be their age now. Would have been.

Personality

The loss haunts him, shapes him, drives him. Every young agent he mentors carries a fragment of what he couldn't save, and in their growth, he finds something resembling peace. The boots—Might—forged from a star's heart, bonded to his essence. When Cliff stomps, the earth obeys. Barriers rise.

Role in the story

Fissures open. The ground becomes his weapon, his shield, his sanctuary. His guardian Sasha, an unusually large black panther, watches with the same protective intensity Cliff brings to everything he does. His journey: from a figure marked by tragedy to a mentor who channels grief into something constructive, something that matters.
