Vega

Holy Item

Summary

A celestial broadsword forged from fallen star fragments

Description

Vega, a broadsword bonded to Goaden Reeves. Forged from celestial fragments. Its blade, adorned with a star-shaped hole, seems to whisper of the cosmos from whence it came.

Appearance

Vega is a magnificent broadsword, embodying the celestial power and tragic beauty of its origins. The blade, approximately four feet in length, is forged from the fragments of a heavenly star, giving it a lustrous, almost otherworldly appearance. The metal, named Stellarian in honor of its celestial origins, has an iridescent quality, shimmering with hues of silver and blue, reminiscent of a star-studded night sky.

Near the hilt, the blade features a unique tribal star-shaped hole, a symbol of its divine heritage. This star void is surrounded by intricate patterns and engravings that seem to shift and change in the light, echoing the star's fragmented journey from the heavens to earth.

The hilt is wrapped in a material that gleams like liquid starlight, providing a firm and otherworldly grip. The crossguard extends gracefully, its design mimicking the celestial paths of stars in orbit, and it is inlaid with small, sparkling stones that resemble distant stars.

Origins

Crafted by the hand of Fate herself, Vega is a testament to a cataclysmic event in the heavens - the shattering of a star. This celestial tragedy, which reverberated through the cosmos, resulted in the star's fragments cascading down to earth. These fragments, imbued with divine power and the essence of the universe, were collected by Fate. With divine intervention, she wove these shimmering shards into the creation of Vega, infusing the sword with a fraction of her own boundless power.

The tribal star-shaped hole near the hilt symbolizes the star's fragmentation and the profound connection between the celestial and the mortal realms. The engravings along the blade are not mere decorations but are imbued with the essence of Fate's will, making Vega not just a weapon, but a conduit for divine power.

Legacy

Vega, like the other holy items forged from the star's remains, is a gift of divine intervention, meant to guide and aid those strong-willed souls chosen by Fate. Its creation marks a pivotal moment in the history of the universe, where the divine touched the mortal plane in a tangible way.

In the hands of Goaden, Vega finds its true purpose, serving as a beacon of celestial might and a reminder of the fragile balance between the heavens and the earth. It is a symbol of the responsibility that comes with wielding such power, a call to use its strength with wisdom and reverence.

Abilities

  • Energy Channelling
  • Celestial Manifestation
  • Silver Flame Amplification

Wielder

Goaden Reeves - Vega was gifted to Goaden by Fate when he was 8 years old, choosing him as its wielder through divine providence.
