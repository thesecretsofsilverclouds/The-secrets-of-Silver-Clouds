About the Author

Silent is a debut author whose passion for storytelling has been a lifelong source of solace and inspiration. Born and raised in Slough, England, Silent faced significant challenges early in life, including childhood trauma, homelessness, and financial hardship. Through these difficult experiences, they discovered the transformative power of writing as a means of self-expression, healing, and connection with others. Over the past decade, Silent has poured their heart and soul into crafting "The Secrets of Silver Clouds," a novel that draws upon their personal experiences to explore universal themes of resilience, friendship, and the indomitable strength of the human spirit. The story follows the journey of Goaden Reeves, Ashai, and their companions as they navigate a fantastical world filled with magic, adventure, and the constant struggle between light and darkness. As a self-taught writer, Silent has worked tirelessly to hone their craft, seeking out resources and support from the writing community to develop their skills and refine their storytelling. They have participated in online writing workshops, attended conferences, and connected with fellow authors who have provided valuable feedback and encouragement throughout the creative process. Silent's writing style is characterized by rich, immersive world-building, complex and relatable characters, and a deep exploration of the human experience. They draw inspiration from a wide range of genres, including fantasy, Anime, science fiction, and adventure, blending elements of each to create a unique and compelling narrative. Beyond their passion for writing, Silent is an avid reader, a nature enthusiast, and a strong advocate for mental health awareness and support. They believe in the power of storytelling to foster empathy, understanding, and connection among people from all walks of life. "The Secrets of Silver Clouds" represents the culmination of Silent's creative journey and their desire to share a powerful and transformative story with the world. They invite readers to join them on this incredible adventure and to become an integral part of bringing this novel to life.

Thank you for embarking on this journey with me...

About the Book

Imagine if urban fantasy and action adventure had a wild night out in London, stumbled into a comic book shop, and emerged with a hangover and superpowers. Nine months later, The Secrets of Silver Clouds was born, an illegitimate love child, raised on a steady diet of manga and British wit. Step into a world where the lines between reality and fantasy blur, where magic pulses through the veins of London's streets, and where the fate of the city rests in the hands of unlikely heroes. "The Secrets of Silver Clouds" is an electrifying urban fantasy novel that combines the gritty allure of gangster life with the awe-inspiring spectacle of epic anime storytelling.

Prepare to be transported into a realm where ancient powers, secret societies, and Demonic entities collide in a battle for supremacy. At the heart of this struggle lies Ashai Bennet, a young woman gifted with celestial powers, and Goaden Reeves, a rebellious swordsman with a mysterious past. Together, they navigate a labyrinthine web of treachery and magic, forging alliances with the notorious Demon's Legion and the enigmatic agents of MI6.

As the shadows of a malevolent immortal loom over London, Ashai and Goaden must confront their own demons and unravel the secrets of their intertwined destinies. With each turn of the page, you'll find yourself immersed in a vividly realized world, populated by a cast of unforgettable characters - from the charismatic leader of the Demon's Legion, Truth Hughes, to the haunted and powerful Shonen Kartia.

"The Secrets of Silver Clouds" is a masterful blend of urban fantasy, gangster grit, and anime-inspired action. Author Silent weaves a tapestry of magic and intrigue that will keep you on the edge of your seat, yearning for more. The novel's breakneck pacing, stunning set pieces, and profound character arcs create an immersive experience that feels like watching an epic anime movie unfold before your eyes.

This groundbreaking novel defies genre conventions, appealing to fans of urban fantasy, gangster dramas, and anime alike. Silent's prose crackles with energy, painting a vivid picture of a London transformed by magic and mayhem. The author's cinematic storytelling and deft handling of complex themes make "The Secrets of Silver Clouds" a must-read for anyone seeking a fresh, exhilarating take on the fantasy genre.

Don't miss your chance to embark on this unforgettable journey. Discover the secrets that lie hidden within the pages of this extraordinary novel. Join Ashai, Goaden, and the Demon's Legion as they fight to save London - and themselves - from the forces that threaten to tear their world apart. "The Secrets of Silver Clouds" is more than just a book; it's an invitation to step into a realm where anything is possible, and where the power of friendship, love, and sacrifice can change the course of destiny.

Codex

Demon's Legion Codex Entry: The Demon's Legion is a tight-knit group of young, rebellious individuals with unique abilities and backgrounds. United by their shared experiences and a strong sense of loyalty, they operate as a vigilante group in London, often taking on dangerous missions to protect the city and its inhabitants.

Notable Members: Goaden Reeves (Former Leader): A skilled swordsman and wielder of the Holy Item, Vega. Goaden possesses a strong presence and the ability to manipulate silver flames.

Truth Hughes (Current Leader): Truth has the power to manipulate light and is known for his strategic thinking and strong leadership skills.

Rose: A nature-based magic user with the ability to control vines and plants. Rose is a fierce fighter and a loyal friend.

Gabriel: With his blood wings and the ability to create blood projectiles, Gabriel provides aerial support during combat.

Damien and Marley (The Dread Twins): These brothers are known for their perfect synchronicity in battle, making them a formidable duo.

Anarchy: Sharing a soul with the Demon Balthazar, Anarchy has access to Demonic powers and is a crucial member of the team.

Headquarters: The Demon's Legion operates from a hidden base in London, which serves as a sanctuary for its members and a place to plan their missions.

Symbolism: The group's iconic bandanna, adorned with intricate mandala designs and floral motifs, serves as a symbol of their unity and shared purpose. Wearing the bandanna during battles is a sign of their commitment to each other and their cause.

Goaden Reeves: A force of nature wrapped in human form, Goaden stands as a pillar of strength within the Demon's Legion. His tall, athletic frame belies the tempest that rages within, a storm of power and emotion barely contained beneath his cocksure exterior. Dark curls frame a face etched with the scars of battles fought and won, while his eyes burn with an intensity that speaks of both fierce determination and hidden vulnerabilities. As former leader of the Demon's Legion, Goaden's influence ripples through the group like an undercurrent, his presence a lodestone that draws both allies and enemies into his orbit. His path intertwines inexorably with that of Ashai Bennet, their shared destiny a tapestry woven from threads of love, loss, and the unyielding desire to protect a world teetering on the brink of chaos.

Abilities: Wielder of Vega, a celestial broadsword that sings with the power of fallen stars. Goaden's mastery over silver flames sets him apart, his presence a beacon of raw, untamed energy that can turn the tide of any conflict. His prowess in combat is matched only by his innate ability to inspire loyalty in those around him.

Guardian: Kai, a diminutive dragon with scales of obsidian and ivory. Despite his small stature, Kai's wisdom spans eons, serving as both confidant and conscience to his impulsive ward. Their bond, forged in the crucible of shared trials, allows Goaden to tap into reserves of power that defy mortal understanding.

Rose: A living embodiment of nature's untamed beauty and fierce resilience, Rose stands as a verdant oasis amidst the urban chaos of the Demon's Legion. Her lithe form moves with the fluid grace of a willow in the wind, fiery strands of hair cascading like autumn leaves around a face etched with both compassion and steel resolve. Intricate tattoos wind across her bronze skin, each one a living testament to her profound connection with the natural world. Rose's presence within the Legion serves as a grounding force, her earthy wisdom tempering the group's more volatile elements. Yet beneath her nurturing exterior lies a formidable warrior, capable of unleashing nature's fury upon those who threaten the delicate balance she strives to maintain.

Abilities: Rose wields the very essence of nature itself, her body a conduit for primal forces that defy mortal comprehension. Rose can talk to animals and With but a thought, she can coax vines from barren concrete, their thorny tendrils ensnaring foes or providing sanctuary to allies. Her connection to the earth grants her unparalleled healing abilities, capable of knitting flesh and mending spirits with equal efficacy.

Role in the Legion: Rose serves as the Legion's moral compass and healer, her gentle strength a balm to wounded bodies and souls alike. Her unwavering devotion to Truth, tempered by a fierce independence, often places her at the centre of the group's internal conflicts. As the Legion faces increasingly dire threats, Rose's ability to bridge the gap between the natural world and London's urban landscape proves invaluable, her insights offering new perspectives on age-old problems.
