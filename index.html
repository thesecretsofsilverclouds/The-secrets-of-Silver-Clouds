About the Author

Silent is a debut author whose passion for storytelling has been a lifelong source of solace and inspiration. Born and raised in Slough, England, Silent faced significant challenges early in life, including childhood trauma, homelessness, and financial hardship. Through these difficult experiences, they discovered the transformative power of writing as a means of self-expression, healing, and connection with others. Over the past decade, Silent has poured their heart and soul into crafting "The Secrets of Silver Clouds," a novel that draws upon their personal experiences to explore universal themes of resilience, friendship, and the indomitable strength of the human spirit. The story follows the journey of Goaden Reeves, Ashai, and their companions as they navigate a fantastical world filled with magic, adventure, and the constant struggle between light and darkness.

About the Book

Imagine if urban fantasy and action adventure had a wild night out in London, stumbled into a comic book shop, and emerged with a hangover and superpowers. "The Secrets of Silver Clouds" is an electrifying urban fantasy novel that combines the gritty allure of gangster life with the awe-inspiring spectacle of epic anime storytelling.
