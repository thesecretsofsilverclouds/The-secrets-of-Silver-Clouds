Nimbus - The Cloud Rat

Creature

Summary

A mutated lintel transformed by Holy Item Radiation into an adorable yet catastrophically powerful apex anomaly

Description

Nimbus resembles a four-legged lintel whose body mutated under prolonged exposure to Holy Item Radiation. Its fur swirls like living stormclouds, threaded with leafy tufts and tiny blossoms that bloom with each breath. Wide, halo-bright eyes and a plumed tail give it the appearance of a child's toy brought to life—yet this illusion masks an apex anomaly that is equal parts pet, pest, and weapon.

Appearance

Civilians whisper the nickname 'Cloud Rat' after spotting it darting across London's chimneys, leaving trails of mist in its wake. Syndicates place absurd bounties on live capture. MI6's file marks it simply: Classified Hazardous Entity. Nimbus devours spells like candy, burping them back magnified by holy radiation.

Behaviour

It disperses into storm vapour to slip through cracks, engulfs foes in fog, or inflates into a titanic storm-beast silhouette. When overfed on magic, it leaks weather phenomena—sparks from its tail, sudden rain showers, tornadoes from sneezes. In critical overload, it triggers miniature Holy Item Radiation Storms. It purrs like distant thunder, loves shiny objects and crumbs, and hides in coats.

Abilities

In battle, it acts without malice—but its 'play' can level buildings. Nimbus embodies the chaos of the post-Holy Tragedy world: adorable, coveted, impossibly dangerous.

Abilities

  • Spell Swallowing & Reflection (Kirby Effect)
  • Cloud Morphing (Storm Vapor Form)
  • Atmospheric Overcharge (Weather Phenomena)
  • Presence Puff (Aura Shockwave)
  • Arcane Absorption
  • Pocket-Dimension Stomach
  • Digestive Nap (Post-Spell Sleep)
  • Snack Bribery Vulnerability
