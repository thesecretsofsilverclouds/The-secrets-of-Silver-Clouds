Demon's Legion

Faction

Summary

Elite supernatural force protecting London from otherworldly threats

Description

The Demon's Legion stands as London's first line of defense against supernatural threats, an elite group of individuals bonded by shared trauma, unwavering loyalty, and extraordinary abilities. Founded in the aftermath of personal tragedy and tempered by countless battles, the Legion operates in the shadows to keep the city safe from forces most citizens never know exist.

Origins

Once led by Goaden Reeves, the Legion emerged from necessity rather than design. What began as a group of outcasts and warriors seeking purpose evolved into a formidable organization. Their reputation, earned through blood and sacrifice, strikes fear into the hearts of those who would threaten London's fragile peace.

Structure & Operations

The Legion operates with fluid hierarchy, valuing skill and trust over rigid command structures. Each member brings unique abilities to the team, creating a versatile force capable of adapting to any threat. Their headquarters serves as both sanctuary and war room, a place where strategies are forged and bonds are strengthened.

The Legion's Code

Members of the Demon's Legion live by an unspoken code: protect the innocent, trust your team, and never leave anyone behind. This bond transforms individual warriors into a unified force, their combined strength far exceeding the sum of their parts.
