Ashai Bennet

Character MI6

Summary

Gifted with celestial powers and serving as an asset of MI6, Ashai is central to the battle for London's fate

Description

Ashai Bennet is a young woman blessed with extraordinary celestial powers that mark her as pivotal to the cosmic struggle engulfing London. Her journey from an ordinary life to becoming a key figure in the supernatural conflicts defines much of the narrative. Ashai's connection to Goaden runs deeper than mere romance—it's a bond forged in starlight and destiny, their fates intertwined by forces beyond mortal comprehension. Her gentle strength masks a wellspring of power that, when unleashed, can reshape reality.

Personality & Character

Ashai exhibits a strong will and determination in the face of major plot twists or revelations. Despite her emotional nature, she's capable of relying on her skills and the support of those around her. In challenging scenarios, she's unafraid to engage in combat to protect others.

She tends to be reserved around strangers, reflecting her slight social anxiety and secluded upbringing. However, she shows a surprising level of defiance and aggression towards antagonists, especially when protecting those she cares about.

Background

Her fear of the unknown and social anxiety stem from her isolated upbringing. While she is uncomfortable with creepy or unnerving things, she has a deep love and affinity for all creatures, finding comfort in their presence.

When stressed or emotionally troubled, Ashai has a habit of nail-biting, which she consciously tries to control. She finds solace in riding her Rune rider at night, drawing calmness from the stars and the tranquility of the night.

Relationships

Ashai's interactions are characterized by care and protectiveness, especially towards Yukon, whom she views as a younger brother. Her relationship with Cliff is based on respect, akin to a father-daughter dynamic.

Her connection with Goaden Reeves is profound and complex, built on mutual respect and a growing romantic attraction that neither fully acknowledges.

Holy Item: Thyia

Thyia, the Pendant of Life, is an ethereal and mesmerizing artifact crafted by Fate. The pendant is fashioned from translucent material, with myriad light blue stars that shimmer and dance within its core. These stars twinkle and glow, ebbing and flowing with the rhythm of life around it.

Guided by the wisdom bestowed by her guardian Greah, Ashai understands the sacred balance of life and strives to use the pendant's powers to maintain harmony and protect the living.

Abilities

  • Starlight Energy Manipulation
  • Healing Powers
  • Protective Barriers
  • Light-based Attacks

Guardian

Greah - Ashai's guardian is a diminutive, halo-crowned creature whose iridescent dragonfly wings shimmer with prismatic light. Born in the wake of the Holy Tragedy, she is believed to be a fragment of celestial essence given consciousness by Fate herself. Blunt, clever, and mischievous in equal measure, she serves as a sharp-tongued guide whose humour cuts through the fog when others hesitate.
