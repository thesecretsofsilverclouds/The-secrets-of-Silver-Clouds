Lad of Ragnorir

Character Ragnorir Guardian-Bonded

Summary

Viking descendant wielding the storm-forged battleaxe Stormcleaver—a living legend who commands thunder

Description

A true descendant of the Viking warriors of Ragnorir, Lad stands as a living embodiment of the ancient warrior tradition—part berserker, part shaman, wholly unstoppable. Forged in the crucible of northern warfare and tempered by the wisdom of his serpentine guardian Ten, he bridges the savage fury of his ancestors with the strategic brilliance required to lead in the modern age.

Background

When Lad enters the battlefield, the sky responds. Thunder rolls. Lightning cracks. The air seems to bow before him, acknowledging the storm made flesh. His Holy Item, Stormcleaver—a battleaxe forged in the heavens, hums with barely contained energy.

Personality

Crackling with lightning that dances along razor-sharp edges like eager hounds awaiting their master's command. When Lad and Ten fight as one, they become something greater than the sum of their parts—a living legend that strikes terror into Ragnorir's enemies and inspires unwavering loyalty in its warriors. Together, they represent the perfect synthesis of ancient tradition and adaptive strategy, raw power and refined technique, destructive capability and healing grace. Lad of Ragnorir is a warrior, a storm given human form and Viking determination.
