Oblivion

Holy Item

Summary

An Infernal blade forged in the demon realm's depths, containing the tortured soul of a betrayed demon that drives wielders to madness

Description

Forged in the infernal depths of hell, Oblivion carries a dark legacy intertwined with betrayal and malevolent consciousness. Once, a demon named Oblivion stood as a formidable entity in the demonic hierarchy—powerful, ruthless, feared. Then came the plot.

Origins

Rivals ensnared the demon through arcane rituals, binding its essence into molten metal. Infernal smiths, masters of dark weapon crafting, channelled the demon's rage, power, and despair into the blade's core, creating a weapon imbued not only with physical might but with sentient malevolence. The sword pulses with its own will, whispering destruction and paranoia into the minds of those who dare wield it. Its true curse lies in corruption—the demon's consciousness slowly erodes the sanity of its bearer, filling their thoughts with madness until they become nothing but vessels for its dark desires.

Appearance

Yet Oblivion's power extends beyond psychological torture. The blade manipulates and warps reality, transcending physical limitations. Its wielder can unleash devastating attacks that strike through dimensional barriers, hellish energy causing destruction on scales few weapons can match. Jedia the Faceless once claimed it through a demonic pact, trading mortal secrets for this terrible weapon.

Powers

After Shonen Kartia's vengeful confrontation with Jedia, the blade fell into his hands. Shonen, having spent centuries honing willpower and skill, stands as one of the rare few capable of wielding Oblivion without succumbing to its corruption—a testament to his indomitable spirit and the burden of immortality that shields his mind from what would shatter lesser beings.

Abilities

  • Reality Manipulation
  • Dimensional Strike (Transcends Physical Space)
  • Hellish Energy Projection
  • Sanity Erosion
  • Consciousness Corruption
  • Whispers of Destruction
  • Paranoia Inducement
  • Malevolent Sentience

Wielder

  • Shonen Kartia
