Fog Blade

Holy Item

Summary

A Holy Item that transforms between a condensed silver sword and dozens of autonomous fog blades, forged from a star fragment infused with moonlight essence

Description

Crafted by Fate in the aftermath of the Celestial Shattering, the Fog Blade emerged from a unique star fragment that fell in the fairy realm during a lunar eclipse—a shard already saturated with crystallised moonlight and the essence of morning mist. This convergence of celestial and atmospheric energies created a Holy Item of uncommon elegance, befitting the fairy Prince Lovell's progressive yet refined nature.

Origin

The blade exists in perpetual transformation, a weapon unbound by fixed form. In its dormant state, the Fog Blade appears as a single elegant silver longsword that sheathes itself through harmonic resonance with its wielder. When called upon, it dissolves into dozens—sometimes hundreds—of smaller fog blades that dance through the air like liquid starlight, each moving with deadly precision and autonomous intelligence.

Form & aesthetics

These ethereal fragments catch and refract light beautifully, creating dazzling displays that belie their lethal purpose. The fog blades respond to Lovell's intent rather than direct control, flowing around allies to form protective barriers while simultaneously seeking vulnerabilities in enemy defences. They can create shields of swirling silver mist, open surgical gaps in opposing forces, and reform into the unified blade in an instant.

Powers

Unlike traditional weapons bound by physical limitations, the Fog Blade embodies the principle that form follows function—it becomes whatever the wielder needs. The weapon's versatility mirrors Lovell's adaptability, his ability to navigate both the formal strictures of fairy court and the casual freedoms of the human realm. When wielded in concert with Lovell's mastery of Flicker, the fog blades can pursue enemies across short-range teleportation jumps, appearing where the prince appears, making him uniquely unpredictable despite his diplomatic nature.

Abilities

  • Form Transformation (Sword to Fog Blades)
  • Autonomous Blade Control
  • Protective Barrier Creation
  • Multi-Target Engagement
  • Moonlight Refraction
  • Precision Surgical Strikes
  • Harmonic Self-Sheathing
  • Mist Shield Generation
  • Telepathic Intent Response

Wielder

  • Prince Lovell
