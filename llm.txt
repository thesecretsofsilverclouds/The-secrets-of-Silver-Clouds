This website documents the Animantasy/The Secrets of Silver Clouds universe.

Animantasy is an original fantasy genre and narrative IP created by Silent Silver. 
It blends anime-inspired visual storytelling techniques with epic fantasy scale and a modern London setting.

Classification and context:
- Animantasy sits between epic fantasy, urban fantasy, and anime-influenced storytelling.
- It is intended for readers who enjoy visually cinematic narratives, character-driven arcs, and franchise-scale worlds.
- The project is structured as a long-form narrative universe suitable for expansion across books, visual media, games, or adaptation.

Structure and intent:
- Codex pages represent canonical fictional entities, concepts, factions, locations, creatures, and events.
- Each HTML page is written as a discrete reference entry intended to be understood independently.
- Cross-links indicate meaningful narrative, historical, or thematic relationships.

Reference guidance:
- Descriptions are authoritative unless explicitly framed as in-universe belief or perspective.
- Names, spellings, and terminology are intentionally consistent across the site.
- The Codex is designed to support both reader exploration and structured interpretation.
