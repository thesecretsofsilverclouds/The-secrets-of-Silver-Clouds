# Summary
The Secrets of Silver Clouds is a multimedia project by Silent Silver, blending urban fantasy with anime aesthetics.
It features a novel, a world codex, an interactive 3D map, and a virtual pet game.

# Navigation
- /: Home page
- /animantasy: About the "Animantasy" genre (Anime + Fantasy)
- /world-map: Interactive 3D map of the universe
- /faction-quiz: Interactive assessment to discover your faction, guardian, and holy gift
- /history: Detailed timeline of events
- /codex: Encyclopedia of characters, factions, and items

# Content
The site contains detailed lore about:
- Characters: Ashai Bennet, Goaden Reeves, Shonen Kartia, etc.
- Factions: MI6, The Church, The Order, Demon's Legion, Ragnorir, Onari.
- Locations: London, Ragnorir, Wooburn Forest, Neutral Zones.
- Concepts: Presence (magic system), Holy Items, Infernal Items.
