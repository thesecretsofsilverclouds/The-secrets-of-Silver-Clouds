Gabriel - The Blood Angel

Character Demon's Legion

Summary

The Legion's best rapper and third strongest member, wielding his own blood as both weapon and wings

Description

Gabriel cuts an unlikely figure as a warrior—fair-skinned, blonde-haired, and notably shorter than his fellow Legion members—but what he lacks in stature, he more than makes up for in raw talent and explosive power. Known throughout London as the Legion's greatest rapper, Gabriel channels his competitive fire and insecurities into performances that electrify crowds and shake stadiums. His voice carries the weight of street survival and the fury of those cast aside by society. Behind the dramatic flair and loud humour lies a tempestuous spirit quick to anger, especially when reminded of his rank as third strongest behind Goaden and Truth—a fact that ignites cursing outbursts and redoubled determination. Gabriel's true power emerged through holy item radiation exposure similar to Rose's awakening: the extraordinary ability to weaponise his own blood. He sculpts crimson into devastating constructs, most notably the massive wings that grant him flight—earning him the moniker 'Blood Angel.' Often seen in an oversized baggy jacket with the hood pulled low, Gabriel embodies the unpredictable energy of the Legion itself: impulsive, entertaining, fiercely loyal, and absolutely lethal when the moment demands it.
